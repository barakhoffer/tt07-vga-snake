## How it works

A simple snake game with vga output and left,right,up,down buttons.

## How to test

Connect to a VGA monitor. Change left,right,up,down (ui_in[0:3]) buttons to change movement.

## External hardware
[TinyVGA PMOD](https://github.com/mole99/tiny-vga)
